About the game

What is Vilnius Go? 🎮🏃

Run to take over the city. Complete loops around real blocks to claim areas and climb the leaderboard.

Play your way ⚡

  • Solo: go alone and capture at your own pace.
  • Team: join a crew, stack captures, defend together.
⏱️ 1-Hour Window: once you start moving, you have 1 hour to complete a capture (finish your loop and secure the fragment).

Quick start 💡

  • Start tracking → run a full loop.
  • Short blocks = faster captures.
  • Re-run to defend your areas.

Play fair, stay aware. GPS syncs later.
