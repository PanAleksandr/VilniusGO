What is Vilnius Go

Vilnius Go is an open, city-scale movement challenge. You move — run, jog, or power walk — log your distance, and earn points for your team and district. The goal is simple: stay active, discover the city, and climb the leaderboard together.

No special gear is required: any tracker or app is fine. What matters is consistency, safety, and fair play.

How It Works

  • Create or join a team when you register.
  • Move regularly: runs, jogs, power walks — any outdoor distance-based activity.
  • Log each session in your profile; points are calculated automatically.
  • Help your team & district: all your points contribute to both rankings.

Tip: short sessions still count. Aim for 3+ activities per week to unlock the weekly bonus.

Teams & Districts

Teams can be friends, classmates, or colleagues — any motivated group. Each team represents a district of Vilnius, adding extra local rivalry on top of the team race.

  • Team size: up to ~10 active members is recommended for easy coordination.
  • District selection: choose once per season; changes mid-season require admin approval to keep competition fair.

Scoring System

Points are based on distance, frequency, and consistency. Basic rules:

  • Every 1 km = 1 point.
  • Weekly bonus: +5 points for 3+ activities on different days.
  • Consistency streak: 4 consecutive weeks = +10 extra points.
  • Per-session cap: max 20 points, to avoid unhealthy single-day marathons.

Seasons & Leaderboard

The game runs in seasons (typically 8–12 weeks). During a season, points accumulate for both teams and districts. The Leaderboard shows:

  • # — position
  • Team — team name
  • District — represented area of Vilnius
  • Points — total score for the season

When a season ends, stats reset — keeping competition fresh and giving new teams a chance to break into the top.

How to Join

  1. Open the Register page and create your account.
  2. Pick (or create) a team and select your district.
  3. Start logging activities — the more consistent you are, the faster your progress.

Ready to begin? Go to the Register page and join Vilnius Go today. You can also check the current Leaderboard or read the latest news and updates.

Fair Play & Safety

  • Safety first: log only real activities, done without health risks.
  • Honesty: no fake distances or boosting. Admins may review suspicious entries.
  • Respect the city: follow traffic rules, use sidewalks and paths, and keep Vilnius clean.

FAQ

Can I change teams? — Yes, between seasons. During a season — on request, to keep balance.

What devices can I use? — Any app or tracker that measures distance, including a phone or watch.

If I skip a week? — No problem. Your streak resets, but you still earn points for new sessions.
